Why Change Rebounded exists

The meeting ends, you walk out feeling good. You noticed the problem early enough, it’ll get fixed now.

The meeting ends, you walk out feeling like nothing is moving. Five meetings on the same problem. Five meetings where the topic talked about was something else tangentially related at best. The meetings start feeling like meetings for meeting’s sake. You feel stuck.

It’s a familiar pattern we’ve all seen play out. It happens in almost every organisation. Sometimes just certain teams, sometimes multiple divisions, or even the whole organisation. In every case it costs money. In some it costs opportunities. Some become a statistic under ‘businesses closed this year.’

Change Rebounded exists to help you find your ability to change, adapt and grow again. We help you get unstuck so you can put your energy into getting things done.
