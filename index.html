Change your boundaries. Change your future.

Change Rebounded helps organisations notice the invisible boundaries holding important work in place, then reshape them enough to find a way forward.

The search for the right boundaries is a bit like Goldilocks. It is about noticing where boundaries are too rigid, strangling adaptation, or too unclear, deflating decision-making. The space in between is where movement becomes possible again.

We will not sell you the fairytale of “just right”. Perfection usually gives organisations one more reason to wait. Good enough boundaries are often what the work needs to start moving.

Why Change Rebounded exists

Change work can lose shape.

It can have a clear intent, a sponsor and a plan, and still become too loose, too crowded, or too hard for people to act on.

By boundaries, we mean the obvious ones: roles, team responsibilities, reporting lines, governance forums and decision rights. We also mean the ones organisations pay less attention to: assumptions about what is acceptable, cultural norms about how things get done, old decisions that still carry weight, and memories of previous changes that shape what people think is possible now.

Change Rebounded exists to help organisations see those boundaries more clearly, understand what they are doing, and put better boundaries around important work so it can move.

The way we work

Boundaries are not problems to remove.

They are often there for good reasons. They may have helped people feel safe, manage uncertainty, protect relationships, avoid unnecessary conflict, or get work done in conditions that were never as simple as the plan made them look.

So we are careful with them.

We do not come in and move boundaries around people. We work with leaders and teams to notice the boundaries they are already working within, understand what those boundaries are doing, and decide what may need to be clarified, loosened, strengthened or redrawn.

The organisation has to be involved in seeing it. Otherwise the work just becomes another answer brought in from outside, and most organisations already have enough of those.

How we help

Change Rebounded helps leaders start seeing stuck patterns differently, then work with their organisation to put better boundaries around the work.

Boundary Workshop

A facilitated session that introduces leaders to boundary thinking. Using metaphor, reflection and structured inquiry, we help teams explore how visible and invisible boundaries shape behaviour, decisions and change.

Boundary Sprint

A focused two-week engagement for work that needs a closer look. We work with leaders and selected teams to begin uncovering the boundaries, patterns and tensions affecting movement.

Change as a Service

Ongoing consulting support for a limited number of organisations each year. We work alongside leaders as change unfolds, helping them make sense of what is happening, hold the work more clearly, and adjust the boundaries needed for progress.
